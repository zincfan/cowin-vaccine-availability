• Introduction to our concept:-

Streaming the video of an online lecture is quite heavy and requires a good internet bandwidth. So we bring the essence of those videos in a different format, which requires way lesser bandwidth & data. We take snapshots from the video, at a regular interval, which ensure that all major concepts written on the class board are covered.

Unique Methodology

The teaching methodology adopted by us is unique and ensures that education is not compromised because of lack of internet facilities in certain areas.

Top Faculties

The faculties who provide the video lectures have the required knowledge and expertise in their respective fields, ensuring quality of education is not compromised.

Interactive Teaching

We provide a comments section with each lecture, which the students can avail to interact with each other and the instructors, to discuss queries and concepts.


A Simple Guide to our website

We will define the process which any teacher or educator can follow to use our platform, to provide their content to the students living in areas with poor internet speeds. First, the teacher or educator can make an account on our platform, by providing basic information. This can be used to login in future, whenever they access our platform, and will be used to group all their lectures together. Then they get an option to upload their video lectures. As of now we support video lectures of up to 1.8 GB in size. We convert these videos into the format of snapshots with audio. These are then displayed in the format, where students can see the video of snapshots along with audio. A slider with the latest 5 snapshots helps in ensuring that students can follow the content of each snapshot along with the audio. These are made available on our website, which can be accessed by any student, without needing a high speed internet. This system is highly effective in capturing the essence of the video lectures. Students can pause in between lectures, and refer to the snaps to understand concepts being taught. Both the snapshots & audio guide the student to grasp the concepts with ease.

Some Unique Features which make our Website stand out among other Educational Platforms

Low Bandwidth Accessibility

• Students can access Educational Content in our Unique Format, which ensures learning at Low Internet Speed

• Our Platform provides the essence of Video Lectures, in form of Snapshots & Audio, which reduces the bandwidth requirement by 70-80%

Option to upload reference PDFs

• Teachers have the option to upload a PDF along with their videos

• This will be made available for students to download and view

• This can help students better understand the lecture(s)

Impactful both During & Post Covid – 19

• Our platform can help bridge the gap for providing e-Education to Areas with poor internet connectivity
